How It Works

1. Website Scope

Before development can begin, we will need to get on a call to determine which website domain (website address) is desired and if it is available. We'll also talk about the preferred color pallete, formatting, and general layout of the website.

2. Important Information

Once we've established the base layout of the website, I will need all necessary information and pictures that are going to be hosted on the website. For food trucks, this might look like a menu with pictures of all the different food options available to customers. If there are social media platforms that you have created for your business (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, etc.), I can link those pages directly on the website as well.

3. Website Development & Build Time

With all of the specifications and necessary information gathered, I can now begin development of the webpage. On average, it takes approximately 2 to 4 weeks to build out a website. However, build time will greatly depend on the overall size and specifications requested by the owner. I will be able to provide a better estimate once I have an idea of the scope of the website.

4. Features

Features vary depending on the desired specifications of the owner, but generally speaking, you can expect:

1. Landing Page - Main page that customers visit when clicking on your website.
2. Contact Information - Email, Business Phone Number, Social Media Platforms.
3. Menu - Menu displaying all products or services offered by the business.
4. Location - Imbedded Google Maps overlay to show customers the location of your business.
5. Webpage Analytics - All webpage visits can be tracked in real time using Google Analytics. With these metrics that both you and I will have access to, you can see:

Activity - How many people are visiting your page, both in real time and on a daily, monthly, and yearly basis.

Location - The generalized location of your webpage visits. If it is a local business, most visitations will be out of the Austin area.

Recurring Consumption - How much of your business consists of recurring customers. Are you performing better or worse than in previous months?

& So Much More - The analytics homepage can be tailored to meet your desired specifications and metrics that are fundamental to your business. This data will help you become more aware of your customer base and what steps are necessary to make your business grow.

5. Pricing

My web development services adopt a unique pricing model:

Initial build out of the website is Free, and comes with a one month trial to determine whether the website makes sense for your business. Afterwards, if you see the website having a positive impact on sales and overall web presense, a monthly subscription of $10 per month will be instated. Part of this subscription covers website maintenance, adding additional content to the website, and overall domain health. If you are still not satisfied with the website after the first trial month, you can opt out free of charge.

For comparison, most web developers charge anywhere between $1000 and $10,000 to build out dynamic websites. Unfortunately, this is a huge barrier to entry for small businesses, which is why I've adopted a subscription-based service.

6. Get In Contact

If this webpage service sounds like it would be beneficial to your business, get in contact with me. I'd be happy to get on a call and discuss your unique use-case, as well as any additional concerns you may have. Contact For More Information.
